Life-Expectancy across the decades

Life Expectancy at birth is a metric defining the expected/average life-span of an individual from the time they were born.
Life expectancy can be influenced by many aspects of a country's economic ,geo-political and health-centric developments. Hence, a question arises on the amount of affect these facets have on the rise or fall of life expectancy within that country. That is quastion we hope to answer from this chart, to identify a potential pattern or shift across the previous four decades , in order to see how human-life has been affected.
