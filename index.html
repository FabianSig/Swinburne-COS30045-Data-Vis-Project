Life-Expectancy across the decades

Life Expectancy at birth measures the average expected lifespan of an individual from birth. This metric can be influenced by various factors, including a country's economic, geopolitical, and health-related developments.
Consequently, it raises the question of how these aspects impact the rise or fall of life expectancy in a country.
This chart aims to answer that question by identifying potential patterns or shifts over the past four decades, illustrating how these factors have affected human life.
